TODO APP

Organizes your tasks with a list that can make everything much more manageable and make you feel grounded.
The app lets enables you to keep temporary task or everyday's task under the same section.

You can easily line-through the task which is done on a daily basis.Temporary tasks can be deleted once done. Also the users can change the background color of Todos.

The entire project has been built upon Angular Framework and does not require to eload the page evertime.
